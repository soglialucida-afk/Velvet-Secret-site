Reading example

What does an insight look like?

Field: Decision

"Do I stay on the path I am walking now, or is it time for change?"

This card often appears when the decision itself is not the whole difficulty. The heavier part is trying to live too long without choosing.

The Two of Swords shows a state between two possibilities. One part of you wants to move, while another keeps checking whether it is safe to decide.

The card does not tell you which choice is correct. It points to something quieter: delay may now be taking more energy than the decision itself.

The question is not which path has no risk. The question is which path has been calling you for some time.

Key thought

"Clarity does not always arrive before the choice. Sometimes it comes after it."
